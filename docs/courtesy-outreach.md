# Shollu Modern — Courtesy Outreach Email Drafts

This document contains highly respectful, warm, and professional email drafts (in both Bahasa Indonesia and English) that can be sent to **Ebta Setiawan** (original author of Shollu, developer of `ebsoft.web.id`), notifying him about the modernization rebuild.

Attribution and historical tribute are important, and reaching out is a kind gesture to show respect for his original work (2004–2012) which served a generation of Indonesian Muslim desktop users.
